Weekly Diet Plan (Balanced and Nutritious)

Monday

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with almond milk, chia seeds, and fresh berries.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: 1 banana and a handful of almonds.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and olive oil dressing.
  • Evening Snack: Green tea and roasted chickpeas.
  • Dinner: Steamed fish with quinoa and steamed broccoli.

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and whole-grain toast.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek yogurt with a drizzle of honey.
  • Lunch: Brown rice with lentil curry and a side of cucumber salad.
  • Evening Snack: 1 apple and peanut butter.
  • Dinner: Grilled tofu with sautéed zucchini and carrots.

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, almond butter, and plant-based protein powder.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: A handful of walnuts.
  • Lunch: Whole wheat wrap with hummus, grilled vegetables, and falafel.
  • Evening Snack: Roasted sunflower seeds and a cup of herbal tea.
  • Dinner: Stir-fried shrimp with whole-grain noodles and bok choy.

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Avocado toast on multigrain bread with a poached egg.
  • Mid-Morning Snack: 1 orange and a handful of pistachios.
  • Lunch: Baked salmon with sweet potato mash and sautéed spinach.
  • Evening Snack: Mixed berry parfait with low-fat yogurt.
  • Dinner: Lentil soup with a side of whole-grain bread.
