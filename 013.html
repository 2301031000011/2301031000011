Gujarati Cuisine Menu

This menu is based on traditional Gujarati cuisine, offering a delightful mix of flavors and textures.

Monday

  • Breakfast: Thepla with chhundo (sweet mango pickle).
  • Lunch: Gujarati kadhi, khichdi, and a side of papad.
  • Evening Snack: Fafda with jalebi.
  • Dinner: Baingan bharta with bajra rotla and garlic chutney.

Tuesday

  • Breakfast: Poha with a sprinkle of sev and lemon.
  • Lunch: Undhiyu with puri and shrikhand.
  • Evening Snack: Khaman dhokla with green chutney.
  • Dinner: Moong dal with rice and stuffed bhindi.

Wednesday

  • Breakfast: Handvo with curd.
  • Lunch: Dal dhokli with a side of salad.
  • Evening Snack: Masala chaas with bhakharwadi.
  • Dinner: Sev tameta nu shaak with rotis and rice.

Thursday

  • Breakfast: Methi na dhebra with pickle.
  • Lunch: Chana masala with jeera rice and paratha.
  • Evening Snack: Patra with tamarind chutney.
  • Dinner: Dudhi na muthiya with green chutney.

Friday

  • Breakfast: Gathiya with masala chai.
  • Lunch: Ringan (brinjal) and potato shaak with bajra roti and curd.
  • Evening Snack: Khandvi with coconut garnish.
  • Dinner: Masoor dal with rotis and a side of kachumber salad.

Saturday

  • Breakfast: Sev usal with pav.
  • Lunch: Gujarati dal with rice and a side of methi thepla.
  • Evening Snack: Ganthiya sev with raw papaya salad.
  • Dinner: Handvo with green chutney and buttermilk.

Sunday

  • Breakfast: Dudhi na thepla with sweet chutney.
  • Lunch: Bharela ringan (stuffed brinjal) with bajra rotis and dal.
  • Evening Snack: Jalebi with tea.
  • Dinner: Pulao with curd and a side of fried papad.
